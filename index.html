Our Heritage

Gozal Tea was established in the year 1970, with a simple idea to encourage the world to make a fresh start, every day. Synonymous with quality and culture, our range of widely loved products i.e. Leaf Teas, Green Teas, Fruit Teas, and Herbal Teas, are a testament to our commitment to excellence. With values that guide our decisions and actions, we remain true to our founder's vision - to simply make everyday better with a fresh cup of tea!

Our Journey: Cultivating Inspiration Since 1970

At Gozal Tea, we believe every day deserves a fresh start, a moment of pure inspiration. Our journey began in 1970 with a singular vision: to share the timeless art of tea making with the world.

From the pristine, hand-picked leaves nurtured in the finest gardens, to the meticulous crafting that honors vintage traditions, every Gozal blend is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and premium taste. We ensure each cup delivers an aromatic and naturally exquisite experience.

More than just a beverage, Gozal Tea is an invitation to infuse calmness into your day, to find clarity, and to truly 'Start Fresh, Stay Inspired.' It's a daily ritual designed for discerning palates and health-conscious souls alike.

Behind every blend is the dedicated Gozal Family, passionate about bringing you the purest essence of tea, making everyday moments better, one cup at a time.
